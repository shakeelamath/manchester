Tailored Programs for Your Career Growth

International Diploma in Special Needs Education

  • Focus:Specialized training in supporting students with special educational needs.
  • Target Audience: Current teachers, special education coordinators, or professionals seeking to specialize in special needs education.
  • Career Progression:Roles in special education, special education coordinators, further certifications in specific disabilities.
  • Further Study:Advanced diplomas or Master's in Special Education, Educational Leadership.
  • Description:This program equips you with the knowledge and skills to effectively support and teach students with special educational needs. It focuses on inclusive teaching methods, adapting curriculum, and understanding specific disabilities.

International Diploma in Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA)

  • Focus: Specialization in behavioral interventions for children with autism and other developmental disorders.
  • Target Audience: Educators, therapists, or parents working with children with special needs, especially those in behavioral interventions.
  • Career Progression: Certification as a Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA), specialization in behavioral interventions, roles in clinical practice.
  • Further Study:Advanced ABA certifications or clinical practice in special needs.
  • Description:The ABA program teaches evidence-based techniques for behavioral modification and management, particularly for children with autism. Gain skills to implement effective strategies in educational and therapeutic settings.
